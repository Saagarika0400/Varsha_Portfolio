03

Research Portfolio

Oncology

Therapeutic Resistance in NSCLC

Investigated EGFR signaling, EMT pathways, and LPAR-1 expression in TKI-resistant non-small cell lung cancer. Developed resistant cell lines and CDX models, and evaluated nanoparticle-delivered siRNA therapeutics for overcoming resistance mechanisms.

Virology

HPV-Driven Tumor Progression

Mechanistic investigation of papillomavirus-host interactions in tumor initiation and progression using in vivo mouse models. Current research includes immunological profiling and identification of therapeutic targets relevant to gene-based interventions.

Ophthalmology / Diagnostics

Retinopathy Biomarker Discovery

Proteomics-driven biomarker identification in clinical samples (urine, serum, vitreous) from diabetic retinopathy patients. Developed gold nanoparticle lateral flow immunoassays to enable non-invasive, scalable screening for early disease detection.

Infectious Disease

Host-Pathogen Interactions

Transcriptomic analysis of host responses in parasitic and viral infection models. Prior work on Visceral Leishmaniasis including dual-drug nano-liposome formulation and T-regulatory cell immunosuppression during pathogenesis in murine models.
